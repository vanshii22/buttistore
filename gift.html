WE ARE HAPPY TO HELP YOU

From assisting you in placing the order, through design & production, till the product reaches your doorstep, we are here to help you at every step in this journey.

1. What priority should I choose for delivery?
We offer 3 priorities - super express, express and standard. Super-express is available in limited pincodes only. You can choose based on your requirement date and location.
2. Is it possible to request an early delivery?
Yes, you certainly can. Early deliveries, on the other hand, are dependent on the delivery location. So, before and after placing the order, please confirm the feasibility with the support team.
3. How can I get an order status update?
Once the order is placed, it is assigned to one of the process teams. They will contact you via Whatsapp for a preview.
4. Will I be able to preview the finished product before it is shipped?
Yes. We proceed with the final processing of the order only when the customer has approved the preview
5. Can I include a quote with my art purchase? Is it going to be charged?
Yes, you can have a quote added to the painting. Contact our process team via Whatsapp to get that done. It is free of charge
6. How do you choose color tones and backgrounds for images?
The background is given based on Dress Color, Ambiance, and Skin Tone
7. Is it possible to add further customization to my order?
Customizations are feasible in some cases but in some, they might not be possible. Before placing an order, discuss it with the sales team and get confirmation. Customization is normally paid separately.
8.Can I customize my order’s color tone and background? How much would it cost?
Based on the dress color, ambiance, and skin tone, the artist creates the most appropriate background and color tone. However, by alerting the process team, you can request additional options. Each option will cost Rs. 200.
9. What is digital painting and how is it different from traditional painting?
Digital painting is a modern and emerging form of art where the artist uses the modern state to art tools such as graphic tablet and stylus pen instead of acrylic color, canvas, and brush which are mainly used in traditional painting. There is not much difference between the two forms of art except for the tools that the artists employ. Many artists choose digital painting since it is less expensive in the long run (fewer materials) and is required in the fields of animation, illustration, and 2D game art, which are quite a difficult task if we have to choose a traditional painting to perform it.
